# Scaling Playbook

## Pre-Scaling Checklist

Before increasing budget, verify:

- [ ] Positive ROAS for 7+ consecutive days
- [ ] Multiple ad angles validated (not just one winner)
- [ ] Landing page conversion rate stable
- [ ] Tracking and attribution working correctly
- [ ] Backend can handle more volume (fulfillment, sales capacity)
- [ ] Chargebacks/refunds under control
- [ ] Payment processing healthy (no flags)
- [ ] Profit margin covers increased CAC at scale

## The Bell Curve of Scaling (Jeremy Haynes)

```
                    ┌─────────┐
                   /           \
                  /    WARM     \
                 /   AUDIENCE    \
    ┌───────────/                 \───────────┐
    │  NEVER   │    Sweet Spot    │  NEVER    │
    │  BUYERS  │  (where profit   │  BUYERS   │
    │          │   lives)         │           │
    └──────────┴─────────────────┴───────────┘
    Low spend ──────────────────────> High spend
    High ROAS ──────────────────────> Low ROAS
    Low volume ─────────────────────> High volume
```

**At low budgets**: you reach the hottest audience (already wanting a solution). Easy wins, high ROAS.
**As you scale**: you push into colder audiences who need more convincing. ROAS drops but volume grows.
**The goal**: maximize NET PROFIT, not ROAS percentage.

## Scaling Methods

### Vertical Scaling (Increase Budget)

**Rules:**
- Increase 20-30% at a time
- Wait 3-5 days between increases
- Never jump more than 2x overnight
- Monitor CPA for 48 hours after each increase
- If CPA spikes >50%, reduce back and wait

**Progression:**
| Week | Daily Budget | ROAS Expected |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| 1-2 | $30-50 | Learning |
| 3-4 | $50-100 | 5-20x |
| 5-6 | $100-200 | 4-10x |
| 7-8 | $200-500 | 3-7x |
| 9-12 | $500-1,000 | 2.5-5x |
| 13+ | $1,000-5,000 | 2-4x |
| Pro | $5,000-20,000 | 2-3x |

### Horizontal Scaling (Expand Reach)

**New audiences:**
- Broader interests (from niche to adjacent)
- New lookalike sources (purchasers, high-value, ATC)
- Higher lookalike percentages (1% → 3% → 5%)
- Broad targeting (let algorithm find)
- International markets

**New creative:**
- New angles from customer research
- Different formats (video → carousel → image)
- UGC from new creators
- Seasonal/trending hooks

**New channels:**
- Add Google when Meta is profitable
- Add TikTok organic for free traffic
- Email/SMS for retention (zero acquisition cost)
- Organic content for brand building

**New products/offers:**
- Upsells to existing customers
- Cross-sells
- Higher ticket offers
- Bundle offers for higher AOV

## Platform-Specific Scaling

### Meta/Facebook Ads Scaling

**Creative scaling is the #1 lever.**
Meta's Andromeda algorithm rewards creative diversity.

| Scale Level | Active Ads | New Ads/Week |
|------------|-----------|-------------|
| $100/day | 5-10 | 3-5 |
| $500/day | 15-30 | 5-10 |
| $1,000/day | 30-50 | 10-15 |
| $5,000+/day | 50-100+ | 15-20 |

**Budget structure at scale:**
- CBO campaigns (let Meta optimize across ad sets)
- Consolidate winning audiences (fewer, larger ad sets)
- Broad targeting works better at higher budgets
- Keep retargeting proportional (10-15% of total)

### Google Ads Scaling

**Keyword expansion:**
- Add broader match types (exact → phrase → modified broad)
- Discover new keyword themes from Search Terms Report
- Add competitor brand keywords
- Test Performance Max campaigns

**Quality Score optimization:**
- Improve ad relevance (tighter ad group themes)
- Improve landing page experience
- Improve expected CTR (better headlines)
- Higher QS = lower CPC = more traffic for same budget

## Scaling Risks & Mitigation

### Platform Risks
| Risk | Cause | Mitigation |
|------|-------|------------|
| Facebook ban | Scaling too fast, policy violations | Gradual increase, multiple ad accounts, stay in policy |
| Shopify shutdown | High chargebacks | Quality product, clear expectations, customer service |
| Google suspension | Policy violations, landing page issues | Comply with policies, transparent landing pages |
| Payment processor hold | Sudden revenue spike | Gradual scaling, multiple processors |

### Business Risks
| Risk | Cause | Mitigation |
|------|-------|------------|
| Supplier stockout | Unexpected volume | Multiple suppliers, safety stock |
| Quality drop | Rushing to fulfill | Maintain QC process |
| Customer service overload | Volume spike | Hire/automate before scaling |
| Cash flow crunch | Ad spend before revenue | Payment terms, credit line |

### Metric Risks
| Risk | Signal | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| CPA creep | CPA rising 3 weeks straight | New creative angles, review funnel |
| Ad fatigue | Frequency >3, CTR dropping | Fresh creative, new audiences |
| Audience saturation | Reach >70% of audience | Expand audience, go broader |
| Platform dependency | >80% revenue from one channel | Diversify (add Google, email, organic) |

## Scaling Milestones

### $0 → $1K/mo ad spend
- Find ONE profitable ad + audience
- Validate unit economics work
- Install all tracking properly

### $1K → $5K/mo
- Scale winning ad 20-30%/week
- Add retargeting
- Build creative pipeline (3-5 new ads/week)
- Start collecting UGC

### $5K → $20K/mo
- Multiple winning angles
- Full funnel (cold + warm + hot)
- Consider second platform
- Hire/outsource creative production

### $20K → $100K/mo
- Creative team or UGC pipeline
- Multi-platform (Meta + Google minimum)
- Advanced audiences (suppression, layering)
- Email/SMS revenue covering 20-30% of total

### $100K+/mo
- Portfolio of products/offers
- International expansion
- Agency or in-house team
- Brand building alongside performance
