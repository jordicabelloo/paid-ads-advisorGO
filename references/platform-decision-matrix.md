# Platform Decision Matrix

## Decision Tree

```
START: What does your customer do before buying?
│
├── They SEARCH for a solution (Google, "best X for Y")
│   ├── High ticket / local service → Google Search Ads
│   ├── Physical product with search demand → Google Shopping
│   └── SaaS / tool → Google Search + Meta retargeting
│
├── They DON'T KNOW they need you yet
│   ├── Visual / emotional product → Meta Ads (FB + IG)
│   ├── Coaching / course / transformation → Meta Ads
│   └── New category / innovative product → Meta + TikTok organic
│
└── BOTH (they search AND can be interrupted)
    └── Start Meta → Add Google when profitable
```

## Platform Comparison Matrix

| Factor | Google Ads | Meta (FB/IG) | TikTok |
|--------|-----------|--------------|--------|
| **Intent** | High (searching) | Low (scrolling) | Low (entertainment) |
| **Best for** | Capturing demand | Creating demand | Viral reach |
| **Creative needs** | Low (text ads) | High (video/image) | Very high (native video) |
| **Min budget** | $20/day | $30/day | $20/day |
| **Learning curve** | Moderate | High | Moderate |
| **Attribution** | Strong (click-based) | Weaker (view-through) | Weakest |
| **Audience size** | Depends on keywords | Massive (3B+) | Large (1.5B+) |
| **B2B viable** | Yes | Limited | Very limited |
| **Ecommerce** | Shopping campaigns | Core strength | Growing |
| **Local** | Core strength | Decent | Limited |

## Platform Selection by Business Type

### Ecommerce / Dropshipping
- **Primary**: Meta Ads (demand creation, visual products)
- **Secondary**: TikTok organic (free traffic, viral potential)
- **Later**: Google Shopping (capture search demand from brand awareness)
- **Source**: Davie Fogarty framework — build brand on Meta, amplify with organic

### Infoproducts / Courses
- **Primary**: Meta Ads (targeting by interests, lookalikes)
- **Funnel**: Ad → Instant Form or Landing Page → Follow-up
- **Key**: Lead generation focus, not direct sales
- **Source**: Ruger Viladrosa (BBig Media) — Meta for lead gen, nurture to close

### High-Ticket Services / Coaching
- **Primary**: Meta Ads (create demand, build authority)
- **Funnel**: Ad → Survey → Book → Call → Close
- **Key**: Use Brandformance campaigns for authority building
- **Source**: Brandon Willington — Meta for demand creation, Google only for brand protect

### SaaS
- **Primary**: Google Ads (capture high-intent searchers)
- **Secondary**: Meta Ads (retargeting website visitors)
- **Funnel**: Search Ad → Landing Page → Free Trial → Onboarding
- **Key**: Focus on keywords with buying intent

### Local Services
- **Primary**: Google Ads (search + local extensions)
- **Funnel**: Search Ad → Landing Page → Call/Form
- **Key**: Geographic targeting, call extensions, Google Business Profile

## Budget Progression

### Phase 1: Testing ($30-50/day)
- Single platform
- 3-5 ad variations
- 2-3 audience tests
- Goal: find one profitable ad + audience combo

### Phase 2: Validation ($100-300/day)
- Consolidate winners
- Add retargeting
- Test new creative angles
- Goal: consistent ROAS over 14+ days

### Phase 3: Scaling ($500-5,000/day)
- Increase budgets 20-30% every 3-5 days
- Add new audiences (broader, new interests)
- High creative volume (20+ active ads)
- Consider adding second platform
- Goal: maximize net profit

### Phase 4: Diversification ($5,000+/day)
- Multi-platform (Meta + Google + TikTok)
- Brand protection campaigns
- International expansion
- Creative team / UGC creators pipeline
- Goal: reduce platform dependency

## The Simple Test
> Search your product/service on Google. If you see competitor ads, start with Google. If not, start with Meta. — Brandon Willington
