# Business Type Playbooks

## Playbook 1: Ecommerce / Dropshipping

### Overview
Source: Davie Fogarty ($800M+ in ecommerce sales), Ruger Viladrosa (+15M EUR Meta spend)

### Product Selection (12 Criteria)
1. Gross margin >60%
2. AOV >$100
3. Visual element (looks good in ads)
4. Solves a real problem or triggers desire
5. Not easily found in local stores
6. Low market saturation
7. Not seasonal (or plan for seasonality)
8. Lightweight (cheaper shipping)
9. Not fragile
10. No complex regulations
11. Repeat purchase potential
12. Can be branded (not commodity)

### Funnel
```
Meta Ad → Product Page → Cart → Checkout → Thank You
              ↓ (abandoned)
        Email sequence (abandoned cart)
```

### Campaign Structure (Meta)
```
1. Brandformance (10%) — IG profile visits, warm up
2. Prospecting - Interests (30%) — ABO, test interests
3. Prospecting - Lookalikes (20%) — purchasers, ATC
4. Prospecting - Broad (20%) — demographics only, 5M+
5. Retargeting Warm (10%) — brand + product messaging
6. Retargeting Hot (10%) — ATC 30d, ViewContent 30d, DPA
```

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| ROAS | >3:1 |
| AOV | >$100 |
| CVR (product page) | >2% |
| Cart completion | >40% |
| CAC | < gross margin |
| Email revenue | >20% of total |

### Creative Strategy
- UGC primary (authentic feel)
- Before/after if applicable
- Problem → solution narrative
- Lifestyle aspirational
- Product demo/unboxing

### Scaling Triggers
- 3:1 ROAS for 7+ days
- 3+ winning ad angles
- Supply chain validated
- Chargebacks <1%

### Common Mistakes
- General store (50 products, no brand) → gets banned fast
- Stock photos from supplier → kills trust
- Scaling before product validation → burns cash
- Ignoring email marketing → leaving 20-30% revenue on table
- No branded inserts → missing repeat customers

## Playbook 2: Infoproducts / Online Courses

### Overview
Source: Ruger Viladrosa (BBig Media), Growth Operating Blueprint

### Funnel Options

**Option A: Lead Magnet Funnel**
```
Meta Ad → Landing Page → Lead Magnet delivery → Email nurture → Sales page
```

**Option B: Webinar/VSL Funnel**
```
Meta Ad → Registration page → Webinar/VSSL → Sales page → Checkout
```

**Option C: Instant Form (Meta native)**
```
Meta Ad → Instant Form → WhatsApp/Email follow-up → Sales page
```

### Campaign Structure (Meta)
```
1. Lead Gen - Instant Forms (40%) — low friction, high volume
2. Lead Gen - Landing Page (30%) — higher quality, lower volume
3. Retargeting - Leads (20%) — leads who haven't purchased
4. Retargeting - Engagers (10%) — social engagement retargeting
```

### Message by Awareness Level
| Level | Message Approach | Hook Example |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| Unaware | Talk about their world | "The coaching industry changed in 2026..." |
| Problem-Aware | Agitate the problem | "Still struggling to get clients consistently?" |
| Solution-Aware | Show different approach | "Forget cold DMs. Here's what top coaches do..." |
| Product-Aware | Social proof + differentiation | "Why 500+ coaches chose [Course] over [Competitor]" |
| Most Aware | Direct offer | "[Course] — enrollment closes Friday" |

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| CPL | <$10-20 |
| Webinar registration rate | >30% |
| Webinar show rate | >30% |
| Webinar conversion | >5% |
| Email open rate | >25% |
| Sales page conversion | >2% |

### VSSL Framework (Video Sales Letter)
Structure (80/20 rule — 80% value, 20% pitch):
1. Hook + promise
2. Credibility (quick)
3. Problem agitation
4. Teaching moment (actual value)
5. Bridge to offer
6. Offer presentation
7. Objection handling
8. CTA + urgency

Duration: 15-45 minutes depending on price point.

## Playbook 3: Coaching / Consulting (High Ticket)

### Overview
Source: Brandon Willington ($20K/day, $1.5M/mo revenue)

### Funnel: The Three Cs
```
Clicks (Ad) → Conversions (Survey/Form) → Close (Sales Call)
```

### Survey/Form Strategy
Questions designed as micro-commitments:
1. "What's your current situation?" (easy, gets them started)
2. "What have you tried?" (past attempts = pain awareness)
3. "What's your biggest challenge?" (emotional engagement)
4. "Where do you want to be in 90 days?" (vision)
5. "Are you ready to invest in solving this?" (qualifier)
6. Contact details LAST (highest commitment = end of form)

**Red Flag Test**: Include a question that disqualifies bad fits:
- "What's your current monthly revenue?" (under $X = not ready)
- "When do you want to start?" (timeline qualifier)

### Campaign Structure (Meta)
```
1. Testing (ABO, $30-50/day)
   ├── Broad, Ad A
   ├── Broad, Ad B
   ├── Interest 1, Ads A+B
   └── Interest 2, Ads A+B

2. Scaling (CBO)
   └── Winning audiences + all winning ads

3. Retargeting
   ├── Website visitors 7-30d
   ├── Video viewers 50%+
   └── Engagers 30d
```

### Nurture Sequence (Post-Lead)
**The Hammer Strategy** — hit them hard on day 1:
- Email 1 (immediate): Confirmation + set expectations
- Email 2 (30 min later): Testimonial + story
- Email 3 (2 hours later): "Here's what to expect on the call"
- Email 4 (evening): VSSL or video content
- SMS: Booking reminder

**Identity Text** (thank you page): "You're the kind of person who takes action... most people just scroll past but you committed." This alone raised show rates from 60% to 75%.

### Sales Call Structure
1. **Set agenda** (2 min): "Here's how this works..."
2. **Discovery** — be a therapist, not a consultant
3. **Dig deep**: For every answer, ask "Why?" then "Then what?"
4. **The Iceberg**: Surface problem → real problem underneath
5. **Present solution** (only after they've articulated their pain)
6. **Handle objections** with questions, not arguments
7. **Close**: natural next step, not pressure

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Link CTR | 1-2% |
| Landing page CR | 5-10% |
| Booking rate | 30-50% |
| Show rate | 70-75% |
| Close rate | 30%+ |
| CPA | < 1/3 of client value |

## Playbook 4: SaaS

### Funnel
```
Google Search Ad → Landing Page → Free Trial/Demo → Onboarding → Paid
```

### Campaign Structure (Google)
```
1. Search - High Intent (50% budget)
   ├── AG: [Product category] keywords
   ├── AG: [Problem] keywords
   └── AG: [Competitor] keywords

2. Search - Brand (10% budget)
   └── AG: Brand terms (defensive)

3. Display/YouTube - Retargeting (20% budget)
   ├── Website visitors
   ├── Trial users who haven't converted
   └── Demo attendees

4. Meta - Demand Gen (20% budget)
   ├── Lookalikes of best customers
   └── Interest-based targeting
```

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| CAC | < LTV/3 |
| LTV:CAC | >3:1 |
| Trial-to-Paid | 10-25% |
| Payback period | <12 months |
| CPC (Google) | Industry varies |
| Landing page CR | 5-10% |

## Playbook 5: Local Services

### Funnel
```
Google Search Ad → Landing Page → Call/Form → Appointment → Service
```

### Campaign Structure (Google)
```
1. Search - Service Keywords (60% budget)
   ├── AG: "[Service] near me" / "[Service] [city]"
   ├── AG: "[Specific service type]"
   └── AG: Emergency/urgent keywords

2. Search - Brand (10% budget)
   └── AG: Business name terms

3. Local Services Ads (20% budget)
   └── Google Guaranteed badge

4. Display - Retargeting (10% budget)
   └── Website visitors
```

### Key Google Ads Features for Local
- Location extensions (show address)
- Call extensions (click-to-call)
- Google Forwarding Number (track calls)
- Radius targeting (service area)
- Schedule ads to business hours
- Mobile bid adjustment (+20-30%)

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Cost per Lead | Industry varies |
| Call answer rate | >80% |
| Lead-to-customer | 20-40% |
| Customer value | Service dependent |
| ROI per lead | >5:1 |
